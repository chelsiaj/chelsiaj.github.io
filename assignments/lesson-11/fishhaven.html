Hail Storm

Erica Baker

Rain, snow, sunshine, or the wind usually never have us running for cover but hail on the other hand does. Last week the forecast showed hail and we were given hail, usually we do not know when exactly gets going to strike, but at least with this forecast we can be careful not to go on a stroll to the park with our kids or go anywhere were cant be given shelter at any given time. No injury from the weather impacted anyone as we know of, but it still can hurt, and we want to be safe then sorry.

I recommend everyone to bring a umbrella everywhere they go when the see hail on the forecast. Next week we do not see hail on the forecast, but we we do post multiple updates a day on our 5 day weather forecast to let you know what you are up against with the elements. Here is a picture on the right of what happened 3 years ago in this small town as you can see the hail could get quite large, and their were a lot of injurys that day due to people not being prepared. So I we are here to make you safe and prepared.
